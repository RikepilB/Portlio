# Product Requirements Document (PRD) — portfolio-v2 (Refined Colorways)

## 1. Purpose & Vision
The objective is to overhaul richardpillaca.com into an immersive web interface that mimics the physical traits of premium tactile packaging. The platform merges an organic matte sage-grey felt casing texture with brilliant metallic foils. It directly presents your data analysis and full-stack software development projects within a balanced, premium workspace.

## 2. Core Page Mapping
*   **Home Component (`/`):** A dead-center balanced headline layout surrounded by abundant whitespace. It features a felt/suede textile shader overlay mapping a rich sage green or grey-green base, with your name highlighted in dynamic metallic gold foil.
*   **About Workspace (`/about`):** Shifts into a "clean girl" aesthetic. It trades the deep sage green for a soft, matte satin pearl-grey or broken white canvas. The text blocks arrange themselves as an open vision board, bound together by polished rose-gold typography lines.
*   **Gallery Grid (`/projects`):** Keeps your established filter categories ("All", "Data Science", "Research"). Renders your engineering systems using distinct contrast—combining strong matte white sans headers with gold-foiled tag metrics.
